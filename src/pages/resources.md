# Other Resources

Helpful resources for creating court bundles and understanding court procedures.


## Court Rules

The bundles created by BunTool are designed to comply with CPR Practice Direction 32 and other relevant court rules for England and Wales.
## Official Court Resources

### England and Wales HM Court & Tribunals Service
 
- **Civil Procedure Rules (CPR)**: [https://www.justice.gov.uk/courts/procedure-rules/civil](https://www.justice.gov.uk/courts/procedure-rules/civil) including specific rules for Bundles at [Practice Direction 32 paragraph 27 onwards](https://www.justice.gov.uk/courts/procedure-rules/civil/rules/part32/pd_part32#27.1)
- **Judiciary.uk' General Guidance on PDF Bundles**: [https://www.judiciary.uk/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/GENERAL-GUIDANCE-ON-PDF-BUNDLES-f-1.pdf](https://www.judiciary.uk/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/GENERAL-GUIDANCE-ON-PDF-BUNDLES-f-1.pdf)

The bundle needs for different courts and tribunals can differ.

## Legal Advice and Support

If you need legal help or advice, you may find the following resources useful.

- **National Pro Bono Centre**: [https://www.nationalprobonocentre.org.uk](https://www.nationalprobonocentre.org.uk), and especially their [List of Pro Bono Organisations](https://www.nationalprobonocentre.org.uk/about-pro-bono/pro-bono-organisations/)
- **AdviceNow** How to prepare a trial bundle and index [https://www.advicenow.org.uk/get-help/going-court/small-claims-and-civil-court/how-prepare-trial-bundle-and-index](https://www.advicenow.org.uk/get-help/going-court/small-claims-and-civil-court/how-prepare-trial-bundle-and-index)
- **Citizens Advice**: Free, confidential advice - [https://www.citizensadvice.org.uk](https://www.citizensadvice.org.uk)
- **Support through Court** offering non-legal support and guidance before, during, and after court [https://www.supportthroughcourt.org/](https://www.supportthroughcourt.org/)
- **Finding Legal Options for Women Survivors** for women at risk or experiencing domestic abuse: [https://www.flows.org.uk/](https://www.flows.org.uk/)
