# Contact

## Get in Touch

If you've found BunTool helpful, or encountered a problem, or have any ideas about how to use it, I'd love to hear from you! Please [drop me a line by email to tris@sherliker.net](mailto:tris@sherliker.net).

## Bug reports and software contributions

If you have found a bug or have any contributions, please consider reporting it on [GitHub](https://github.com/TrisSherliker/buntool2) or by email.

## Donations

If you find BunTool valuable, consider supporting its development with a small donation: [Ko-fi donation link: https://ko-fi.com/buntool](https://ko-fi.com/buntool). I intend donations to be used to help cover:

- Hosting costs and bandwidth
- Development costs 
- Ongoing maintenance and upgrades

## Response Times

I read every email sent about BunTool but cannot always respond immediately. Please remember that BunTool is maintained by a small team of one person, so response times may vary. Please also avoid asking for legal advice on this platform, as it is very much separate to my legal work.
